Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit for an ADU in Los Angeles?

Yes. Every ADU in Los Angeles County requires building permits, plan review, and final inspections. New Cali Construction handles the full permitting process end-to-end - architectural design, engineering, city submissions, and inspections - across Culver City and all LA-area municipalities.

How much does an ADU cost in Culver City or Los Angeles?

ADU garage conversions in Culver City and the Westside typically cost between $80,000 and $180,000 depending on square footage, finish level, and utility upgrades. Detached ADUs with new foundations can run $200,000 to $350,000+. We provide free in-home estimates with a written line-item scope and a locked price.

How long does an ADU build take?

A garage conversion ADU typically takes 3-5 months from permit approval to final inspection. Detached new-build ADUs run 5-8 months. We share the full schedule before Day 1 and deliver weekly photo updates throughout.

Can I rent out my ADU for income?

Yes. California ADU law allows homeowners to rent ADUs as long-term rentals (30+ days). New Cali Construction builds every ADU to current code so it's legally rentable upon final inspection. Many Culver City and Westside homeowners use ADUs as rental units, home offices, or guest houses.
